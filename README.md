# CLTV-Analysis
This project expands my previous end-to-end e-commerce analytics workflow by integrating a complete Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) prediction and segmentation pipeline using the theLook eCommerce public dataset on BigQuery.
Key Features

CLTV Modeling with BG/NBD & Gamma-Gamma
Applied probabilistic models to estimate long-term customer value, including purchase frequency, expected transactions, and monetary predictions.

Customer Segmentation (Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze)
Structured segmentation based on predicted CLTV, enabling deeper understanding of high-value vs low-value customer cohorts.

Multi-level Business Performance Analysis
Trend analysis across yearly, quarterly, and monthly horizons to identify growth drivers and seasonal patterns.

LSTM-Based Revenue Forecasting
Neural-network forecasting to understand how projected customer value aligns with overall revenue trends.

Behavioral Insights & Strategic Recommendations
Highlighted customer behavior patterns and provided actionable strategies for:

Retention & churn prevention

Targeted upselling

Marketing prioritization

Long-term business planning
